Packaging

Shape-molded end caps, inner-packs and self-contained shippers of expandable polystyrene (EPS) and expandable polypropylene (EPP), can protect manufactured products of all kind. DPC molds a wide variety of inexpensive, high-performance cushioning, packaging parts from expandable polystyrene (EPS). Its properties for structurability and energy absorption, combined with low unit cost, make EPS a common choice for product protection. Parts molded of expandable polypropylene (EPP) offer a high degree of cushioning for fragile and/or heavy items, and because of their durability and resilience, can be reused, making EPP an excellent material for dunnage or returnable packaging. If volume of your product does not justify the expense of purchasing a tool so that DPC can custom mold a packaging part to fit your application, you might want to consider one of our stock packaging items.
